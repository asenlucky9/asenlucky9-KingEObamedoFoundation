## Design Notes

### Color Usage:
- **Navy Blue**: Headers, navigation, primary buttons (secondary), text accents
- **Orange**: Primary CTAs (Donate buttons), highlights, hover states
- **White**: Backgrounds, card backgrounds, clean spaces

### Typography Hierarchy:
- **H1**: Page titles (32-48px)
- **H2**: Section titles (24-32px)
- **H3**: Subsection titles (20-24px)
- **Body**: 16px base, readable line-height
- **Small**: 14px for captions, metadata

### Spacing:
- Consistent padding: 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px
- Section spacing: 64px-96px between major sections
- Card padding: 24px

### Interactive Elements:
- Buttons: Rounded corners (8px), hover effects, clear states
- Cards: Subtle shadow, hover lift effect
- Forms: Clear labels, helpful error messages
- Links: Underline on hover, color change

### Responsive Breakpoints:
- Mobile: < 640px (single column, stacked)
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px (2 columns, adjusted spacing)
- Desktop: > 1024px (full layout, multi-column)
